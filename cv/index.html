Profile

I am a Chartered Engineer (CEng) and Senior Engineer specialising in gas-turbine whole-engine performance, systems engineering and R&D for industrial power and propulsion systems. I combine first-principles physics and applied mathematics with component-level understanding, real-machine testing and experimental measurement across thermodynamics, aerodynamics, vibration, instrumentation and industrial imaging. I also develop engineering software and deployable AI/ML systems that turn complex models and test data into practical, traceable and auditable engineering decisions.
