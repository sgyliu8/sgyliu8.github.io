01 / About

Engineering across physics, measurement and software.

My career has followed one consistent question: how can difficult physical phenomena be converted into reliable engineering evidence?

I began in aerospace engineering and UAV aerodynamics, developed gas-turbine performance and diagnostics software at Cranfield University, and built wireless measurement systems for rotating machinery at Politecnico di Milano. Today, at Siemens Energy, I contribute to industrial gas-turbine R&D through digital methods, data-informed analysis and advanced measurement.

I enjoy work that connects a physical problem to a repeatable experiment, a defensible model and, ultimately, a tool that engineers can trust.
