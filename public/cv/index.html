Profile

I am a Chartered Engineer (CEng) and Senior Engineer working in thermal power and propulsion R&D. My engineering background combines applied mathematics and physics-based modelling with thermodynamics, mechanical and aerodynamic design, CFD, vibration, experimental mechanics and measurement. I also develop industrial-imaging methods and deployable AI/ML systems, translating models and test data into practical, auditable engineering decisions. International experience in the United Kingdom, Italy and China connects first-principles analysis with physical testing and engineering implementation.
