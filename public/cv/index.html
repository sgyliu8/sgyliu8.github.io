Profile

Chartered Engineer (CEng), registered through the Institution of Mechanical Engineers (IMechE), and a Senior Engineer in gas-turbine R&D. Work spans thermodynamic cycle and performance analysis, industrial imaging and image analysis, deployable AI/ML algorithms, and engineering software and system architecture. International experience in the United Kingdom, Italy and China connects physical modelling and experimentation with practical, auditable engineering tools.
